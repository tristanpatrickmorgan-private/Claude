# Photo Book — 2025 album

Yearly photo book project — currently building the 2025 edition (~300 photos curated on date nights, duplicates already removed).

## Format target

- ~300 photos → roughly **80–120 pages** at typical 3–5 photos per page
- Coffee-table / heirloom format (this will become a yearly series)
- Hardcover, A4 or square, premium paper

## Supplier shortlist (researched 19 May 2026)

### Tier 1 — Premium / heirloom quality (~1,500–3,000 SEK)

**Saal Digital** ([saal-digital.com](https://www.saal-digital.com/photo-book/))
- Considered top quality in independent reviews — sharp prints, premium paper, acrylic / leatherette / metal cover options
- Pro line is the benchmark
- Professional layout software (desktop) — gives full control, steeper learning curve
- Ships from Germany to Sweden
- Best fit if you want a "this is a real book" heirloom feel

### Tier 2 — Best balance, Swedish favourite (~700–1,500 SEK)

**CEWE Fotobok** ([cewe.se](https://www.cewe.se/))
- **"Best in test 2026" in Sweden** (bast-i-test.se, test.se, Prisjakt) — rated 4.3 on Trustpilot from 1,200+ reviewers
- 9 formats and sizes (A4, square, panoramic), hardcover/softcover, multiple paper types
- Free desktop app (best for laying out 300 photos with control) or browser editor
- Reliable Swedish-language support, fast delivery
- **Recommended starting point** — best balance of quality, control and price

**Once Upon** ([onceupon.photo](https://onceupon.photo/))
- Swedish brand, app-based (phone only — no desktop)
- Beautiful design language, very premium feel out of the box
- Limited customisation (no crop, fixed layouts) — strength is the curated aesthetic
- Best for "I want this to just look great" with minimal effort
- Hardcover ~1,500–2,500 SEK for a yearly album

### Tier 3 — Easy / budget (~300–700 SEK)

- **Smartphoto** ([smartphoto.se](https://www.smartphoto.se/)) — Swedish service, lots of formats
- **Photobox** — UK service, ships to Sweden, frequent discounts
- **Snapfish** — similar tier
- Trade-off: paper and binding quality lower than CEWE; fine for a quick / gift book, not for a yearly heirloom

## Recommendation

**Start with CEWE Fotobok** for the 2025 album.

Why:
- Best balance of quality, customisation and price for ~300-photo book
- Desktop app gives proper control over layout (important when curating 300 images, not letting AI auto-flow)
- Swedish support + delivery
- Easy to do again every year with the same template — consistency across the series
- Estimated cost: ~800–1,200 SEK for A4 hardcover, ~100 pages, premium paper

**Upgrade path:** if the first CEWE book feels not-quite-heirloom enough, jump to Saal Digital for 2026.

**Sideways path:** if you want to test how it feels in your hands before committing, order a small (40-page) test album from CEWE first — costs ~400 SEK and you get to feel the paper, binding, print quality before doing the full 100-page version.

## Process suggestion (for this year and going forward)

1. Decide format (A4 portrait vs square — square is more "coffee-table", A4 better for landscape mix)
2. Pick paper (matte vs gloss vs premium silk — silk hides fingerprints, matte is most "book-like")
3. Decide hardcover style (printed cover image vs leather/linen with title only)
4. Use CEWE desktop app: import 300 photos → use "auto-layout" as a starting point → tweak page by page
5. Add a short title page + month dividers for chapters (Jan, Feb, ...) — gives structure
6. Captions: light, just a place + a sentence. Don't over-write.
7. Print preview → order
8. Once 2025 is done, save the template — 2026 will be much faster
